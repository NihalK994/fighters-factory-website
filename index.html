About Us

Founded in 2019 by Arjun Gajmer and Anurag Mangar, The Fighters Factory is a combat sports and fitness academy located in Upper Bagdogra. The academy focuses on developing fighters, improving fitness, and building discipline through professional training.

The gym provides structured programs in Boxing, Kickboxing and Mixed Martial Arts for both competitors and fitness enthusiasts.
